What is "Shingeki no kyojin"

The fantasy anime series "Attack on Titan" is based on the manga of the same name by Hajime Isayama. For many years, humanity has been fighting the Giants - huge creatures with incredible strength and rather weak intelligence. The Giants ate people by the thousands, and received untold pleasure from this. And then one day, the survivors manage to create a wall around their settlement, which even the bloodthirsty Giants could not overcome. A hundred years have passed, all this time humanity lived a peaceful and calm life, being confident that they were under the protection of the wall. But one day, a terrible event occurred, witnessed by a boy named Eren and his adopted sister Mikasa. Suddenly, out of nowhere, a super giant appeared, which destroys a section of the protective wall, thereby opening the way for the invasion of his other brethren. Monsters begin to attack the city, and in front of the children, one of them eats their mother. It is at that moment that Eren vows to avenge the entire human race.
